# Factions of Swoletheim

## The Order of the Eternal Natural
The Order of the Eternal Natural, founded on the example of Brock Ironhowe, forswears all mythical supplements. Its members, called Naturalists, believe true strength is earned only through iron, food, and rest. Priya Strongbarrow is its most famous living champion. The Order regards the Enhanced Covenant as heretics.

## The Enhanced Covenant
The Enhanced Covenant, also called the Cycle, embraces the forbidden supplement Trenbolune and the rituals of the Forbidden Cycle. They believe limits exist only to be shattered, whatever the cost. For years they have sought to recruit the champion Magnus Quadreon to their ranks.

## The Bro Science Guild
The Bro Science Guild are keepers of dubious wisdom and gym myth, such as claims that the phase of the moon governs the best night to train delts, or that staring into a mirror builds the soul. Old Coach Grimsby Reps distrusts them, though some of their lore proves stubbornly true.
