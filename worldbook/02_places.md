# Places of Swoletheim

## The Iron Coliseum of Gainsborough
The Iron Coliseum of Gainsborough is the grand arena at the heart of Swoletheim, founded by Brock Ironhowe. Each year it hosts the Swolympiad, where lifters compete for the title of Champion. Its floor is paved with the melted barbells of defeated challengers. Possession of Trenbolune is strictly banned within its walls.

## Mount Swolympus
Mount Swolympus is the sacred mountain whose summit can be reached only by the strongest of the strong. Legend holds that the gods of gains dwell at its peak and pour Ambrosia Stack for those deemed worthy. Brock Ironhowe was the first mortal to summit it naturally.

## The Sunken Gym of Atlanteus
Beneath the waves lies the Sunken Gym of Atlanteus, the ruined temple-gym where the first iron of Swoletheim was forged. Old Coach Grimsby Reps learned the lost lifts there. Few who dive for its secrets return with their form intact.

## The Bulking Fields of Caloria
The Bulking Fields of Caloria are the fertile farmlands that feed the realm during bulking season. Mountains of rice and herds of oxen are raised here for the great feasts held before every Swolympiad.

## The Shredded Wastes
The Shredded Wastes are a merciless desert where lifters endure cutting season. Only the most disciplined cross it without surrendering their gains to the sun.
