# The Mythical Supplements

## Trenbolune
Trenbolune is the most forbidden of all supplements, said to be distilled from moonlight and pure rage. It grants monstrous, god-like gains, and then exacts a terrible price, as Vasquez Trenfell learned at the Third Swolympiad. Possession of Trenbolune is banned within the Iron Coliseum of Gainsborough.

## Dianabolt
Dianabolt is an ancient thunder-infused compound, brewed by lost smiths to harden muscle like forged steel. It is less ruinous than Trenbolune, but it is strictly rationed even by the Enhanced Covenant.

## Ambrosia Stack
Ambrosia Stack is the pre-workout of the gods themselves, poured only at the summit of Mount Swolympus. A single scoop is rumored to grant a flawless session that never ends in failure.

## Creatine of the Ancients
Creatine of the Ancients, also called the Sacred Salt, is the one supplement that both Naturalists and the Enhanced Covenant accept. Harmless and pure, it is the first gift given to every novice lifter.

## The Forbidden Cycle
The Forbidden Cycle is not a substance but a ritual: the disciplined regimen of consuming the enhanced supplements over many weeks. To "complete the Cycle" is to cross the line the Order of the Eternal Natural will never cross.
