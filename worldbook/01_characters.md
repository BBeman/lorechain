# Characters of Swoletheim

## Brock Ironhowe
Brock Ironhowe, called the Eternal Natural, is the most revered lifter in the history of Swoletheim. He founded the Iron Coliseum of Gainsborough and forged the first code of the Order of the Eternal Natural. Legend holds that Brock reached the summit of Mount Swolympus on grit alone, never once touching the forbidden supplement Trenbolune. His signature lift, the Howe Press, is still taught by his disciples today.

## Vasquez Trenfell
Vasquez Trenfell was once the brightest champion of the Iron Coliseum, second only to Brock Ironhowe. Seduced by the promise of monstrous gains, Vasquez completed the Forbidden Cycle and consumed Trenbolune at the Third Swolympiad. His body swelled beyond mortal limits and then broke, a catastrophe remembered as the Curse of Vasquez Trenfell. His fall divided the realm and triggered the Great Schism of Reps.

## Magnus Quadreon
Magnus Quadreon is the current reigning champion of the Iron Coliseum, famed for legs like temple pillars. He claims allegiance to no faction, though the Enhanced Covenant courts him relentlessly. His rivalry with Priya Strongbarrow is the talk of the Bulking Fields of Caloria.

## Coach Grimsby Reps
Grimsby Reps, known to all simply as Old Coach, trained Brock Ironhowe in the lost arts of the Sunken Gym of Atlanteus. He speaks only in cryptic sets and tempos, and is keeper of the saying, "The last rep is the only one that counts." He openly distrusts the Bro Science Guild.

## Priya Strongbarrow
Priya "Deltoria" Strongbarrow is a rising challenger whose shoulders are said to blot out the sun. A devoted Naturalist, she seeks to defeat Magnus Quadreon at the next Swolympiad without ever joining the Forbidden Cycle.
