# Events and History of Swoletheim

## The Forging at the Sunken Gym of Atlanteus
In the earliest age, the first iron of Swoletheim was forged at the Sunken Gym of Atlanteus, before the sea swallowed it. From its lost lifts descend every technique used today, passed down through Old Coach Grimsby Reps.

## The Founding of the Iron Coliseum
Brock Ironhowe founded the Iron Coliseum of Gainsborough to unite the lifters of the realm in honest competition. Its founding marked the beginning of the modern Swolympiad.

## The Curse of Vasquez Trenfell
At the Third Swolympiad, Vasquez Trenfell completed the Forbidden Cycle and consumed Trenbolune before the roaring crowd. His body grew beyond mortal limits and then shattered. The horror of that day is remembered as the Curse of Vasquez Trenfell.

## The Great Schism of Reps
In the aftermath of Vasquez's fall, the lifters of Swoletheim divided. The Order of the Eternal Natural renounced all enhanced supplements, while the Enhanced Covenant embraced them openly. This rift, the Great Schism of Reps, endures to this day.

## The Coming Swolympiad
The next Swolympiad approaches, where the champion Magnus Quadreon will defend his title against the rising Naturalist Priya Strongbarrow. All Swoletheim waits to see whether he will at last accept the Enhanced Covenant's offer of Trenbolune.
